# Design System: AcadMap Visual Transformation
**Project ID:** 12008903255229521340

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
The design system bridges the gap between rigorous institutional stability and the kinetic energy of a student-led collaborative hub. The brand personality is **Intellectual, Reliable, and Empowering**. The visual style is **Corporate / Modern** with a focus on high-performance SaaS aesthetics. It prioritizes clarity through generous whitespace, a structured typographic hierarchy, and subtle depth.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
- **Deep Indigo (#3730a3)**: Foundation of trust and academic authority. Used for primary actions and brand components.
- **Vibrant Emerald (#10b981)**: Denotes growth, success, and positive actions (e.g., "Upload Resource").
- **Amber (#f59e0b)**: Used for highlights, achievements, and cautionary notifications.
- **Cool Background (#f8f9ff)**: A cool, light gray for the base canvas.
- **Pure White (#ffffff)**: For interactive surfaces to create natural separation without heavy borders.
- **Deep Navy Text (#0b1c30)**: For primary headings and body text to ensure maximum readability.

## 3. Typography Rules
- **Font Family**: Inter (standard for legibility in data-heavy environments).
- **Headlines**: Tighter letter-spacing and heavier weights to command attention.
- **Body Text**: Generous line-height to facilitate long-form reading.
- **Labels**: Smaller, all-caps treatment to distinguish supportive information.

## 4. Component Stylings
* **Buttons**: Deep Indigo background with white text. Success actions use Emerald green. Features a subtle 1px top-light highlight for a tactile, modern SaaS feel. Roundedness: **rounded-lg (1rem)**.
* **Cards/Containers**: 1px subtle border (#e2e8f0) with soft, multi-layered shadows. Background: pure white. Roundedness: **rounded-xl (1.5rem)**.
* **Inputs/Forms**: Large tap targets with a 0.5rem radius. Focus states use a 2px Deep Indigo ring with a light blue offset.

## 5. Layout Principles
- **Grid**: Desktop follows a structured 12-column grid with 24px gutters.
- **Whitespace**: Generous whitespace to maintain an "uncluttered" SaaS aesthetic. md (24px) for most container padding and xl (48px) for vertical section spacing.
- **Depth**: Ambient shadows and tonal layering are preferred over harsh borders.
